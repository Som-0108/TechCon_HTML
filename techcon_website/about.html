Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated annual tech conferences worldwide, bringing together thousands of innovators, creators, and thinkers.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to empower the global tech community by fostering innovation, inclusivity, and continuous learning. We aim to bridge gaps in technology, create spaces for collaboration, and showcase groundbreaking work that impacts the world.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lin

AI researcher and advocate for ethical machine learning. Her keynote in 2022 addressed the future of human-centered artificial intelligence.

Jordan Malik

Founder of DevNet and open-source contributor. In 2021, he inspired the crowd with his story of scaling grassroots developer communities worldwide.

Sophia Chen

CTO at NextLeap Robotics. Her groundbreaking work in AI-powered robotics was a highlight of TechCon 2023.
